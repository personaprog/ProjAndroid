Criar icons de Programas Instalados

a:~$ cd .local/share/applications

a:~/ .local/share/applications$ gedit NomeProgram.desktop
 
                  [Desktop Entry]
                  Version=1.0
                  Type=Application
                  Name= <Nomeprogram>
                  Icon= <endereco/icon.png
                  Exec= <"endereco/executavel" %f
                  Comment= < comentarios > 
                  Categories= < categorias >
